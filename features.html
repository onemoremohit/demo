1. User Onboarding & Profiles

Name, age, gender, location, travel preferences. Quick interest/personality quiz to assess "vibe". Option to join solo or as a group.

2. Vibe Matching Engine

Matches based on quiz, preferences, budget & schedule. Supports real-time and async matching. ML-based or rule-based logic.

3. Live Chat & Icebreakers

Brief chats or games to break the ice. Timer-based interaction. If they vibe, both can tap "Let's Trip!"

4. Trip Recommendation Engine

Destination suggestions based on interests, location, budget, weather. Integrates travel APIs like Skyscanner, Amadeus, etc.

5. Trip Planning Module

Itinerary builder, accommodation and activity planner. Collaborate in real time. Option to book directly via the app.

6. Safety & Verification

ID verification, optional social profiles, and emergency contact options to ensure safe meetups and travels.

7. Post-Trip Review & Community

Review companions, share photos, and plan future trips together. Build a travel community through shared experiences.
